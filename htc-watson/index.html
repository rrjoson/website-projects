What is BIKE 2 RUSH?

BIKE 2 RUSH is a Filipino movement to help save the environment from dangerous emissions caused by motorists, esepecially in the congested cities. We aim to provide a social friendly platform where everyone can actively take part in choosing a much greener commuting alternative.

MEET

Meet like minded people who wants to help save the environment. Be part of the community of world changers.

SAVE

Save the environment and save on your fare. Why be stuck in traffic when you can help the environment from dangerous green house gases and a cheaper transportation alternative?

BIKE

Stay healthy and fit. Take back the time spent in traffic and use it for exercise while getting home much faster.
